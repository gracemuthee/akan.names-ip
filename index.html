AKAN NAME GENERATOR!

WELCOME! This is a web application that is able to generate your akan name based on your day of birth and gender. Akan names are based on Ghanian culture.Those born in Ghana are given their first name as a 'day name' .This means they are named after the day of the week they were born.

To find out what your Akan name is, proceed to filling the form below and clicking the generate name button.
